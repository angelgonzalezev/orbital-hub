# How we talk about Orbital

This is the editorial policy behind every word Orbital publishes — landing pages, docs, posts on X, and in-app copy. We keep it public so anyone can hold us to it. The principle is simple: we describe the product as it is, and the [Vision](/vision) as a direction.

## Canonical positioning

Two lines, copy-paste ready.

**Today:**

> Orbital is where Solana startups get discovered — verified profiles, market signals, and direct founder contact.

**Direction:**

> Orbital is becoming the marketplace where Solana startups raise funding, find buyers, and hire.

**Rule:** the direction line never appears on its own. It always sits next to a today-anchor or explicit roadmap framing, so nobody mistakes the destination for the current product.

## Say in present tense

These features are live. Describe them plainly, no hedging:

- Discover and showcase Solana startups
- Verified, human-reviewed listings
- Market signals
- Raising funds — as a founder-declared signal
- Open to acquisition conversations
- Direct founder contact
- Featured listings — pay in USDC to feature your startup
- Embedded wallet
- The Orbital (the 3D globe)

## Say only with future framing

These are on our [Roadmap](/roadmap), not in the product. Every mention must be wrapped in "planned", "on our roadmap", "we are building toward", or "the vision is":

- Raise funding on Orbital
- Buy or sell startups
- Acquisition listings and offers
- Deal rooms and escrow
- Investor accounts
- Intro requests
- Hiring on Orbital

::: info Encouraged, with framing
We want to talk about where Orbital is going — in vision and roadmap contexts, these topics are encouraged. What is banned is stating any of them as a present-tense product claim.
:::

## Never say, in any tense

::: warning Hard bans

- "Instant acquisition"
- On-chain startup sales described as a current capability
- Anything implying Orbital verifies fundraising claims, holds funds in escrow, or brokers deals
- "Vetted deals" or "investment opportunities" — listings are not investment recommendations, and this wording creates legal exposure
  :::

## Signal and word choices

| Instead of                       | Write                               |
| -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| "Open to acquisition" (in prose) | "Open to acquisition conversations" |
| "Ad"                             | "Featured listing"                  |
| "Vetted"                         | "Human-reviewed"                    |

"Raising funds" is self-declared by the founder. Orbital displays the signal; it never endorses it. Copy must not suggest otherwise.

## The litmus test

Before publishing any sentence about the product, ask:

::: tip
Could a user screenshot this sentence and reasonably believe they can complete that action in the app today? If yes, the feature must be live.
:::

If a sentence fails the test, either the feature ships first or the sentence gets future framing. There is no third option.
