# Featured listings

Featuring is a fixed-price visibility boost for a published startup: **20 USDC for 7 days**. Buying again while your feature is active extends the current window, so you never lose remaining time.

::: tip The only payment on Orbital
Featuring is currently the only payment in the product. Listing a startup is free, browsing the [marketplace](https://orbitalhub.dev/startups) is free, and contacting founders is free.
:::

## What featuring buys

| Benefit        | Where it shows                                                                           |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Top placement  | Featured startups sort first in the marketplace, ahead of every filter and search result |
| Gold marker    | Your startup's marker on The Orbital, the 3D globe, turns gold                           |
| Featured badge | Your listing carries a featured badge while the window is active                         |

Placement applies across the whole marketplace — see [Discover](/guide/discover) for how sorting and filters work.

## How the payment works

The purchase is a plain USDC transfer on Solana, signed with your own wallet. There is no card checkout and no intermediary holding your funds.

- **Embedded wallets** sign silently — a single click completes the purchase, with no popup to approve.
- **External wallets** show a normal approval prompt in your wallet app before the transfer goes out.

Before activating the feature, Orbital verifies the payment on-chain. The amount and the payer are checked against the actual transaction on Solana, and a transaction can never be counted twice. Once verification passes, your featured window starts immediately.

## No USDC yet?

You can fund your wallet without leaving Orbital. The built-in onramp accepts card payments and exchange transfers, and it is available in two places:

- directly inside the purchase flow, when your balance is short
- from the wallet panel on your dashboard profile, any time

If you signed in with email or Google, an embedded Solana wallet was already created for you on first login — see [Getting started](/guide/getting-started) if you have not set one up yet.

## Duration and renewals

One purchase buys 7 days. There is no subscription and no auto-renewal: when the window ends, your listing simply returns to normal sorting. Buy again at any point — before or after expiry — to feature it again.

::: info Today vs. roadmap
Gas-sponsored (feeless) transactions for embedded wallets are built and awaiting activation — status: in progress. Broader marketplace transactions such as escrow and settlements are vision territory, not something Orbital does today. See the [Roadmap](/roadmap) for where payments are headed.
:::
